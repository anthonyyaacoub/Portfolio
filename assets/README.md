# Assets Directory

## Images

### Required: Professional Headshot

Place your professional headshot here as `headshot.jpg`:

**Specifications:**
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Size**: 800x800px minimum (square aspect ratio)
- **Background**: Neutral, professional
- **Attire**: Business casual or formal
- **File name**: `headshot.jpg`

**Why this matters**: The Spanish job market expects professional photos on CVs and portfolios. A high-quality headshot signals professionalism and cultural awareness.

**Tips:**
- Use natural lighting or professional photography
- Smile naturally
- Ensure the image is sharp and high-resolution
- Avoid selfies or casual photos
